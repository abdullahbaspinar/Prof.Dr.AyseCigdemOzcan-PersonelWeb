About Me

I am a Professor at the Department of Mathematics of the Faculty of Science of the University of Hacettepe in Ankara, Türkiye. I have been the head of the department from 2021 to 2024. I was an Editor-in-Chief at Hacettepe Journal of Mathematics and Statistics from 01.08.2018 to 30.07.2021. I am a member of the editorial board of the International Electronic Journal of Algebra and the Istanbul Journal of Mathematics.

My research interest lies in Ring and Module Theory, recently:

  • Lifting of some kind of elements, such as idempotents, regular and unit regular elements
  • Stable Range Condition

I supervised 2 PhD and 2 Master students. I have currently 1 master student.

Area of Interest

  • Ring and Module Theory:
  • Small modules
  • Z*(.) Functor
  • Torsion theories (co)generated by certain classes of modules
  • V-rings (rings in which every simple module is injective)
  • GV-rings (rings in which every simple singular module is injective)
  • Semiregular, Semiperfect, Perfect rings and modules
  • Harada rings and modules
  • Quasi-Frobenious (QF) rings
  • Clean Rings
  • Stable Range
